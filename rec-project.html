Sunnyside Scrambler

Created a ride entry with CU Theme Park Engineering and Design club for the 2026 Ride Engineering Competition hosted in Hershey Pennsylvania. The ride is a small scale ride for candy riders that is able to have rotation, vertical displacement of 1ft, and achieve 2G's of acceleration. Additionally, the ride is required to be fully ASTM F2291 compliant.

My Role

  • Coordinated communication between teams
  • Prepared design review materials

Engineering Contributions

  • Control panel design & fabrication
  • Ride assembly and troubleshooting
  • Fabrication of scissor lift and control panel parts
  • Laser cut and engraved information plates to be mounted to ride and control panel

Ride Engineering Competition - The Sunnyside Scrambler

Worked with CU Theme Park Engineering and Design Club to create a small scale attraction for candy riders. My role was as an assistant project manager, which included ensuring communication between subteams, documenting and preparing for design reviews, and creating a full line-by-line ASTMF2291 compliance document.
